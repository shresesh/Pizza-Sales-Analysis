# Pizza Sales Analysis: End-to-End Business Intelligence

## 🍕 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of pizza sales performance to identify key business indicators and growth opportunities. By leveraging **SQL Server** for data extraction and **Power BI** for visualization, the analysis monitors essential KPIs, identifies peak sales periods, and categorizes menu performance to drive data-driven decision-making.

## 📊 Key Indicators & Business Insights
* [cite_start]**Revenue & Volume:** Successfully tracked total revenue, average order value, and a total of **49,574 pizzas sold**[cite: 2, 3].
* [cite_start]**Temporal Trends:** Identified that **Friday and Saturday evenings** exhibit the highest order volume, while **July and January** are peak months for sales[cite: 2].
* [cite_start]**Category Intelligence:** Discovered that the **Classic Category** contributes most significantly to total sales volume[cite: 3].
* [cite_start]**Product Performance:** Isolated the **Top 5 and Bottom 5 pizzas** by revenue, quantity, and total orders to optimize menu engineering[cite: 2, 3].

## 🛠️ Technical Toolkit
* [cite_start]**SQL Server:** Developed complex queries to calculate metrics such as **Average Pizzas Per Order** and **Daily/Monthly sales trends**[cite: 2, 3].
* [cite_start]**Power BI:** Built an interactive two-page dashboard featuring a **Home Page** for KPIs and a **Best/Worst Sellers Page** for product-level analysis[cite: 1, 2].
* [cite_start]**Data Transformation:** Performed data cleaning and formatting of order dates and times to ensure temporal accuracy[cite: 2].

## 🖥️ Dashboard Features
* [cite_start]**Executive Summary:** High-level cards for Revenue, Average Order Value, and Total Pizzas Sold[cite: 1, 2].
* [cite_start]**Trend Visualizations:** Daily and Monthly charts to pinpoint high-demand periods for staffing optimization[cite: 1].
* [cite_start]**Market Share Analysis:** Pie charts showing the distribution of sales by pizza category and size[cite: 1].
* [cite_start]**Product Leaderboard:** Dynamic bar charts showcasing the best and worst-performing menu items[cite: 1, 2].
